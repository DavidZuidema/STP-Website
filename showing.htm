Where's The Rich Magic?

If you're in the Ann Arbor area, you've probably heard of Rich Sheridan and his amazing software factory Menlo Innovations. Rich is a man of extraordinary character and kindness, and when we were trying to get our own start-up going, Rich agreed to mentor us. When we showed Rich the video we had made, he liked it so much he asked us to do one (or three =D) for Menlo. We really excited to be working for a company that makes such cool software.

So we started poking around Menlo's site to see how they were using the web to tell their story. The Menlo we knew came mostly from hearing Rich tell his favorite anecdotes about clients and staff. His stories were some of the best parts of our time together. Alas! There was hardly a trace of Rich's magic anywhere to be found on Menlo's site! More people needed to see what Menlo was really all about, and they needed to hear it from Rich! But Rich is only one man with only so much time. Our mission was clear: choose the best Menlo stories, narrated by Rich, and bring them to life!

With these videos, it's a lot easier for all of us to share little pieces of Rich. We love these stories because they encapsulate the essence of Menlo. These are the ones that stick with you; that you re-tell to others.

Let Us Find Your Magic.

Or better yet, let us tell stories about your client's successes. That's the real secret.

When you're in the trenches, fighting to build a successful company, often times it becomes hard to see the forest from the trees. We know, we've been there; but that's why we're here, to help you lose the MBA-speak and tell your story so it sounds like there's actually a human being behind it. Send us an email. We'd love to hear about what you're working on and just maybe we can help.
